Photography for ABERCROMBIE

"ABERCROMBIE"

New-York, New-York USA

2017

Objective

This picture was taken for the famous brand Abercrombie&Fitch as part of a photography competition.

The objective was to transpose the special atmosphere of their store which made the fame of this brand.

Details

The picture has been shot in their New-York store so I had to compose with the environment;

On one hand the picture should have been better with some additional lights and a real studio setup but on the other, the fact that I took this picture "Spontaneously" allows to bring more authenticity to the picture.

Concerning the composition, I asked my models to came into this stairwell which is architecturally pretty interesting. It may be the heritage of the ancient building which have been carefully restored by the firm to fit in their artistic direction.

In post production I added some grain to remind the analog photography which match pretty well with the vintage style of the place.

Corporate identity for PETITE FAIM

"PETITE FAIM"

Dijon, Bourgogne France

2018

Objective

I had to create an entire corporate identity based on the name, the business sector and the location.

The one I chosen is named "Petite Faim" and is based into the «République» district in Dijon. It's appears perfect to me because this area hosts a lot of students and the food delivery particularly speaks to them these times.

Details

By creating this corporate identity, I had to care about the visual part as well as the values. Concerning the values, since République is a student district, I thought that youth should be a great first value.

Furthermore, all the companies in this business sector offer pretty the same pricing. What make the difference is the customer relation and in this very youth quarters it’s only improving this.

Then an other value that appears essential to me was the care about the environment because it's getting more and more important and it’s becoming a sales pitch especially for the youth target audience.

Concerning the visual, Food delivery is a business sector which grows alongside with the web and the mobile apps so I picked their design codes and adapted them to a logo.

Regarding to the values I've chosen, the visual identity had to speaks to the youth which are very accustomed with mobile and web. By using the Web Design codes this must works pretty well.

The result is a logo with very round curves and pretty bold strokes. It is composed of three different elements: a house, a chef’s hat inside this house and three bold strokes besides. The house and the chef’s hat refer to the business sector because Petite Faim delivers food at home and the three bold strokes refers to speed.

Concerning the color, I chose this bright green that refers to the environment which Petite Faim tries to protect.
