Photography for ABERCROMBIE

"ABERCROMBIE"

New-York, New-York USA

2017

Objective

This picture was taken for the famous brand Abercrombie&Fitch as part of a photography competition.

The objective was to transpose the special atmosphere of their store which made the fame of this brand.

A special thanks to my models Antoine rousset, Romain Braccini, Pabl Parnaso and Martin Gaboriaud

Details

The picture has been shot in their New-York store so I had to compose with the environment;

On one hand the picture should have been better with some additional lights and a real studio setup but on the other, the fact that I took this picture "Spontaneously" allows to bring more authenticity to the picture.

Details

Concerning the composition, I asked my models to came into this stairwell which is architecturally pretty interesting. It may be the heritage of the ancient building which have been carefully restored by the firm to fit in their artistic direction.

In post production I added some grain to remind the analog photography which match pretty well with the vintage style of the place.
